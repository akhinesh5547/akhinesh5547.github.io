Welcome to Admissions

Welcome to Canadian University Dubai's admissions page. Here you will find information on how to apply to our university, admission requirements, deadlines, and more.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form on our website.
  2. Submit the required documents, including transcripts, test scores, and any additional materials requested.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Wait for a decision on your application.
  5. If accepted, follow the instructions provided for enrollment.

Admission Requirements

Admission requirements vary depending on the program and level of study. Please refer to the specific requirements for your program of interest on our website.

Deadlines

Application deadlines also vary by program. Be sure to check the deadlines for your desired program on our website and submit your application before the specified date.
