About Canadian University Dubai

Discover our mission, values, and the impact of our education.

Our Story

Founded in 2006, Canadian University Dubai (CUD) is a forward-thinking institution offering a range of academic programs inspired by the Canadian curriculum. Located in the heart of Dubai, our university provides students with an education that prepares them to be leaders in a globalized world. Our commitment to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement ensures that our graduates are well-equipped to meet future challenges.

Mission and Vision

Mission

To provide a gateway for students to pursue higher education, research, and professional development through a Canadian-based curriculum in a culturally diverse and globally engaged environment.

Vision

To be recognized as a leading university that fosters innovation, creativity, and academic excellence, contributing to the development of future leaders who will make a positive impact on society.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our actions.
  • Respect: We embrace diversity and promote an inclusive environment where everyone is treated with dignity.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in our academic programs, research, and community engagement.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and the pursuit of new ideas that can lead to positive change.
  • Collaboration: We foster a spirit of cooperation and teamwork among our students, faculty, and partners.

Why Choose CUD?

At Canadian University Dubai, we provide a unique blend of academic rigor and practical experience. Our students benefit from:

  • A diverse and inclusive campus community.
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities and resources.
  • Opportunities for international exchange and study abroad programs.
  • A strong network of industry connections and career services.
  • Supportive faculty and staff dedicated to student success.
